Personal Life

Cristiano Ronaldo’s role as a father is one of the most cherished aspects of his life. Beyond his fame and footballing success, he often describes his children as his greatest motivation and source of happiness.

Ronaldo became a father for the first time in June 2010, when his eldest son, Cristiano Ronaldo Jr., was born. The identity of the mother has never been made public, as Ronaldo chose to keep that part of his life private. He has full custody of Cristiano Jr., who has grown up under his care and often appears alongside him at matches, award ceremonies, and training sessions. Like his father, the young Ronaldo is already showing immense passion and talent for football.

In June 2017, Ronaldo welcomed twins — Eva and Mateo — through surrogacy. Later that same year, in November 2017, he and his partner Georgina Rodríguez, a Spanish model and influencer, welcomed their first child together, Alana Martina.

Ronaldo and Georgina’s relationship began in 2016, when they reportedly met at a Gucci store in Madrid. Since then, Georgina has been a constant presence in Ronaldo’s life — not only as a partner but also as a supportive figure for his children. She frequently accompanies him to events and has become a public personality in her own right.

In April 2022, the couple experienced both joy and heartbreak when Georgina gave birth to twins — a boy and a girl — but sadly, their baby boy passed away during childbirth. Their surviving daughter, Bella Esmeralda, remains a bright light in their lives. Ronaldo later shared that the tragedy was one of the most painful moments of his life, but the love and strength of his family helped him endure it.

Today, Ronaldo and Georgina live together in Saudi Arabia with their children. Despite his global fame and demanding career, Ronaldo is known to be a devoted and hands-on father, often sharing glimpses of his family life on social media — from training sessions with Cristiano Jr. to vacations with the entire family. To Ronaldo, family isn’t just a part of his life — it’s the foundation that keeps him grounded, motivated, and grateful for everything he has achieved.
